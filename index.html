About Our Studio

ModernStudio Architecture Studio is a collaborative research and design lab working at the intersection of nature and digital fabrication. Founded in 2020, we explore spatial structures that adapt organically to their physical environment.

Through kinetic components, material research, and algorithmic design models, we construct environments that minimize resource usage while maximizing human and environmental well-being.
